# Report Sections

## Required

- Basic information: project, period, reporter, report date.
- Data statistics: commits, contributors, completed tasks, in-progress tasks, meetings, action items.
- Weekly overview: short grounded summary.
- Development progress: git-derived feature, fix, refactor, perf, docs, or task progress.
- Task completion: completed and in-progress work.
- Meetings and action items: attended meetings, decisions, assigned actions.
- Problems and blockers: current obstacles and solutions when available.
- Next week plan: concrete upcoming work.

## Recommended

- Key outcomes: top completed deliverables.
- Risks and coordination: risks, dependencies, people or teams needed.
- Growth and knowledge sharing: learning, practices, reusable notes.

## Personal vs Team Report

Personal reports filter all sections to the selected member.

Team reports include one member block per detected or requested member, plus shared decisions and risks.
